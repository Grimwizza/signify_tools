# Signify Brand & Style Guidelines

> Extracted from the official Signify PowerPoint template 2025 and font package.
> Use this file as the reference whenever working on Signify-branded materials.

---

## 1. Brand Identity

**Company:** Signify N.V. — the world leader in lighting.

**Core mission statement (use verbatim in presentations):**
> Signify is the world leader in lighting. We provide professional customers and consumers with quality products, systems and services. Our connected lighting offerings bring light and the data they collect to devices, places and people. Our innovations contribute to a safer, smarter more sustainable world.

**Contact format:** `firstname.lastname@signify.com`

---

## 2. Logo

Three approved logo variants, all based on the wordmark "Signify":

| Variant | Usage |
|---|---|
| **Full-color (gradient)** | Preferred on white/light backgrounds. Gradient runs from `#00E487` (green) to `#1ECBD0` (teal), left to right. |
| **Dark gray** | Use on light backgrounds when color printing is unavailable. Fill: `#303034`. |
| **White** | Use on dark (`#303034`) or image backgrounds. |

- Never stretch, recolor, or alter the logo.
- Always maintain clear space around the logo.
- The logo symbol is a circular form (the Signify "S" ring) — do not use it detached from the wordmark in digital/presentation contexts without explicit guidance.

## 3. Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Dark Gray** | `#303034` | Primary text, dark backgrounds, logo |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Primary background, text on dark |

### Brand Accent Colors

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Signify Green** | `#00E487` | Primary brand accent — CTAs, highlights, icons, logo gradient start |
| **Mint** | `#66EFB7` | Secondary green, softer highlights |
| **Signify Teal** | `#1ECBD0` | Logo gradient end, supporting accent |
| **Signify Blue** | `#1432FF` | Links, bullet points, secondary CTA |
| **Periwinkle** | `#A1ADFF` | Softer blue, supporting/background accent |

### Neutral Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Medium Gray** | `#ACACAE` | Secondary text, dividers, supporting UI |
| **Light Gray** | `#D5D5D5` | Backgrounds, subtle fills, section headers |

### Usage Rules
- Dark slides use `#303034` background with white text and green (`#00E487`) accents.
- Light slides use white or `#D5D5D5` backgrounds with `#303034` text.
- Green is the hero accent — use it sparingly and purposefully for maximum impact.
- Blue (`#1432FF`) is reserved for links and bullet point markers.

## 4. Typography

### Typefaces

| Font | Role | File (from font package) |
|---|---|---|
| **Signify Raisonne DemiBold** | Headlines, titles, divider text | `SignifyRaisonne-DemiBold.otf` |
| **Signify Relative Book** | Body text, presenter notes, captions | `SignifyRelative-Book.otf` |
| **Signify Relative Bold** | Sub-headings, emphasis within body | `SignifyRelative-Bold.otf` |

> Web formats (`.eot`, `.ttf`, `.woff`, `.woff2`) are available in `Signify_Font_oct2020/Web/`.

### Type Scale (Presentations)

| Element | Font | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Title slide headline (short, 1–2 lines) | Raisonne DemiBold | 60pt |
| Title slide headline (long, 3+ lines) | Raisonne DemiBold | 40pt |
| Divider / section header (short) | Raisonne DemiBold | 80pt |
| Divider / section header (long) | Raisonne DemiBold | 60pt |
| Divider subtitle / supporting title | Raisonne DemiBold | 28pt |
| Slide title (content slides) | Raisonne DemiBold | ~28pt |
| Body / content text | Relative Book | ~18pt |
| Captions, footnotes, presenter info | Relative Book | ~14pt |

### Typography Rules
- Titles are **left-aligned** by default (not centered) except on center-aligned layout variants.
- Line spacing: 90% for headlines; 100% for body text.
- No bold on headline text — weight comes from the DemiBold face itself.
- Body bullet points use `•` in blue (`#1432FF`) at second indent level.

## 5. Slide Layouts

The 2025 template includes 28 named layouts in two variants — **Light** (white/light gray background) and **Dark** (`#303034` background). Use dark variants to create visual contrast at section breaks and for emphasis slides.

### Key Layout Types

| Layout | Background | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Title Slide Dark | `#303034` | Opening slide with gradient logo |
| Title Slide Image | Light gray | Opening slide with full-bleed photo |
| Agenda Slide / Agenda Option 2 | White | Table of contents |
| Section Header (dark / light gray / image) | Varies | Chapter dividers |
| Title and Content / Dark | White / Dark | Standard content slide |
| Text and Image Right/Left | White | Content + photo |
| Text and Image Right/Left Dark | `#303034` | Dark content + photo |
| Large Image Right/Left (Light & Dark) | Varies | Image-dominant layouts |
| Two Content | White | Side-by-side columns |
| Fact Tiles Slide | Dark | Key stats / numbers |
| Quote Slide | Dark | Pull quotes (~30 words) |
| Call to Action | Dark | End-of-section CTA |
| Closing Slide | `#303034` | Thank you / contact |
| Blank / Blank Dark | White / Dark | Custom layouts |

### Photography / Image Guidance
- Use full-bleed images on title and section slides — right-click → Format Background → Picture or Texture fill.
- Select imagery with **natural clear space** so text overlays remain legible.
- Photography style: bright, natural, human-centric, connected to lighting environments.

---

## 6. Iconography & Illustrations

- Use Signify's icon set in **green (`#00E487`) on dark backgrounds** and **dark gray on light backgrounds** for optimal contrast.
- Icons should be placed at a **minimum 15% of their original size** (no tiny icons).
- Maintain consistent line thickness across icons on the same slide.
- Use icons **sparingly and purposefully** — not as decoration.
- Quote mark icon is available in green as a standalone asset.

---

## 7. Footer & Slide Numbering

- Footer format: `[Presentation name] - CONFIDENTIAL` (insert via Insert → Header & Footer).
- Slide numbers appear bottom-right on all content slides.
- Footer text style: Relative Book, small (~10pt), gray or white depending on slide background.

---

## 8. Presenter / Title Slide Info

- Presenter line format: `Presenter Name & Date` — set in **Relative Book** (not Raisonne).
- Closing slide includes: "Thank you!" in large Raisonne, followed by contact line in Relative Book.

---

## 9. Voice & Messaging Principles

Signify communicates with confidence and clarity. Key messaging pillars:

- **Leadership:** "the world leader in lighting"
- **Quality:** professional-grade products, systems, and services
- **Connected:** data from lighting devices enriching places and people
- **Purpose:** contributing to a safer, smarter, more sustainable world

Tone is **professional, forward-looking, and grounded** — avoid jargon, keep sentences direct.
